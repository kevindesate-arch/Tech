About

STUTechWeek is our premier annual academic event dedicated to the celebration of technology and innovation. It's a dynamic platform for students, faculty, and industry professionals to come together, share knowledge, and explore the future of tech.

What to Expect:

  • Technology Exhibits: Explore cutting-edge projects and research from various departments. See firsthand the innovative solutions our students are building.
  • Hands-on Workshops: Participate in interactive sessions led by experts. Learn new skills in areas like AI, cybersecurity, web development, and more.
  • Thrilling Competitions: Test your skills and compete against your peers in exciting challenges, including hackathons, coding duels, and robotics contests.
